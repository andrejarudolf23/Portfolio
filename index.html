ABOUT ME


Hi there! My name is Andreja Rudolf and I'm from Belgrade, Serbia. I am 23 years old and currently a Software Engineering student at Informaton Technology School. Now I am approaching the final stage of graduation.

I have many years of experience creating software, mobile apps and websites mostly for fun. I coded in many different languages such as C, C#, JavaScript, PHP, Swift etc.. However some of my favourite projects I've done are written in PHP. If you would like to take a look at some of them, feel free to skip to my Portfolio section!

In my spare time I like working out, playing sports, chess, reading a lot, coding, watching TV Shows and of course listening to music. My favourite music artist is Eminem and my favourite TV Show of all time is Prison Break.

My main daily goal is very simple. Just work on yourself every day, learn something new and get better than you were the previous day.
